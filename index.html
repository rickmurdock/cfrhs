Mission Statement

The Central Florida Railway Historical Society, Inc. promotes railway heritage preservation and educates its members and the public about rail transportation, its history and impact, with a focus on Central Florida.

Meetings

Held the second Monday of each month (except December) at 7:00 PM in the Central Florida Railroad Museum, 101 South Boyd Street, in downtown historic Winter Garden.

Affiliated with the Winter Garden Heritage Foundation

Member of HeritageRail Alliance
